About The Project

Vasara is a Django-based flashcard learning application designed to make language learning efficient and enjoyable. The name "Vasara" means "Summer" in Lithuanian, evoking a feeling of warmth and enjoyable learning.

Built with a focus on user experience and effective learning methods, Vasara helps you organize your study materials and test your knowledge through interactive quizzes.
