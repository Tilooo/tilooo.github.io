About WINGS

WINGS is an e-commerce platform specializing in beautiful, handcrafted postcards and greeting cards. Our mission is to help people connect through meaningful, tangible messages in an increasingly digital world.

This showcase demonstrates the design and user interface of our Django-powered e-commerce application.

  • User account management

  • Secure checkout process
